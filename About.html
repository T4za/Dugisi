We are Dugisi Technical Services and we Deliver Excellence in Steel Fabrication and Industrial Services.

Engineering Solutions

for a Dynamic World

Dugisi Technical Services is a South African company specializing in steel fabrication, industrial cleaning, repair and refurbishment, machining, hydraulic systems, and more. We are committed to providing the highest level of service to our customers, constantly improving our costs and lead times while maintaining a focus on quality.

Dugisi Technical Services has grown to become a leading provider of industrial solutions in Johannesburg and beyond. Our team of experienced professionals brings together expertise in steel fabrication, engineering, construction, and project management to deliver exceptional results for our clients.

At Dugisi Technical Services, we pride ourselves on our ability to deliver customized solutions tailored to the unique needs of each client. Our diverse range of services includes steel reinforcement, pipes and fittings, industrial cleaning, cylinder reconditioning, valves, and much more. We work closely with our clients to understand their needs, and we use the latest technology and best practices to ensure that every project is completed on time, on budget, and to the highest quality standards.

We are proud to have a broad range of clients across various industries, including Rand Water, Eskom, Transnet, and many others. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as a reliable partner for all of our clients' industrial needs.

If you're looking for a trusted provider of industrial solutions in South Africa, look no further than Dugisi Technical Services. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you achieve your goals.
